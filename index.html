06 / Next two weeks

What we are doing about it

  1. Fix the Meta to GA4 attribution gap

    Implement the Meta Conversions API with offline upload from GA4. This closes the 352 to 11 conversion gap and gives Free 3 Plan's learning phase the signal it needs to scale efficiently.

  2. Consolidate UTM tagging across all Meta ads

    Move every Meta placement to a single source and medium convention. Four fragmented sources are currently hiding the true size of paid social's contribution to the site.

  3. Refresh or pause the Free 3 "Update" creative

    "Free 3 General" outperforms "Free 3 Update" on clicks, cost, and applications in every state it runs. Redirect that budget into a new "General" variant or into scaling the existing winner.

  4. Test Refer a Friend creative as a Meta retargeting layer

    RAF converts at 17% on 2,725 sessions. Serving Meta clickers a follow-up ad built around the referral offer could close the leaky funnel and recover conversions we are currently losing to direct and organic.
